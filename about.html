About Suckless Ratings

Suckless Ratings is a community-driven platform dedicated to discovering, rating, and sharing software configurations that adhere to the philosophy of minimalism, simplicity, and clarity.

In a world of bloated software and resource-heavy applications, we champion tools that respect your hardware and your intelligence. We believe that software should do one thing and do it well.

Our Philosophy

  • Simplicity: Code should be simple and elegant.
  • Clarity: Configurations should be readable and understandable.
  • Frugality: Software should use minimal system resources.
  • Usability: Keyboard-driven workflows are often superior.
